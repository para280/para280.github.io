1. Edit a meeting
  • You can edit subject, start time and duration, document, and description of a meeting you are hosting.
  • If you want to edit a meeting, click the "Edit Meeting" button at the right side of the meeting title that you want to edit.
  • Change information about the meeting. After edit the information, click "Save" button at the bottom of the page.
  • If you don't want to change the information, click "Cancel Edit" button.
  • Your updated meeting information will be appear in your home page, and the updated meeting information notice will be e-mailed to invitees.
2. Cancel a meeting

  • If you want to cancel a meeting that you scheduled, click "Cancel Mtg." button.

  • A red "CANCELLED" mark will be appear on your cancelled meeting, and a cancelled meeting notice will be sent to invitees by e-mail.
  • Once you cancel a meeting, you cannot recover the meeting.
  • If you want to have the meeting again, you have to create a new meeting.
