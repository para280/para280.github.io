  • If you want upload document for the meeting or edit document that you already uploaded, click "Edit Doc." button.
  • Invitee can view or download the uploaded documents before meeting, and you can download these documents if those are not in your local machine while you are in the meeting.
  • If you go to edit document page, software configuration window will be pop-up first.
  • To upload files or URL, you have to click "Yes" and configure and install software first. Or, document edit/upload page will not appear. (To get a guide of "configure software", go to "Register, login and software configuration" page.)
  • An edit document page will be loaded in the page, and you can type the URL that you want to use for your meeting by clicking "Add URL" or upload files by clicking "Add File".
  • When you finish uploading document, click "Save Changes" to upload your document.
  • If you don't want to save the uploading or adding files, click "Revert".
  • After finishing uploading/editing document, click "Home" button to go to your home page.
