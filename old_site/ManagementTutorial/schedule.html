  • To schedule a new meeting, click "Schedule Meeting" button in the left menu bar.
  • a meeting schedule window will pop-up.
  • After fill out fields of meeting information, click the "Next" button at the left bottom to invite people to the meeting that you are creating.
  • To cancel current creating a new meeting, click "Cancel" button at the right bottom of the page.

  • Fill out e-mail addresses of people who you want to invite to the meeting that you are creating, and then click finish at the bottom of the window.
  • If you want to remove one or some of new invitee from the list in the "New Invitee" box, select the invitee(s) that you want to remove and click "Remove" button.
  • To remove all of the invitees on the list, click "Remove All".

  • If your meeting is successfully created, it will go to your home page and your scheduled meeting will be appear under heading "You Are Hosting These Meetings".
  • The meeting invitation to this meeting will be sent to the invitees who are on the invitee list.
