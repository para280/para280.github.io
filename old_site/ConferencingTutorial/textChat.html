    4. Voice chat & Text chat
  • You can talk or have text chat with everyone in the meeting.
  • To talk, press and hold while you are talking or check by clicking the to talk without pressing the button. (To release the "Hands Free" button, uncheck the "Hands Free" by clicking again.)
  • You can control speaker volume from the volume control bar.
  • Your mic volume will be appear under the speaker volume control bar.
  • To use text chat, type in the single line text area at the bottom and click send button. The text chat messages will be appear in the multi line box above the single line box, and everyone who is in the meeting can read it.
  • If you want to send a message not to everybody, use instant massenger by double clicking your name at the top of the participant view.
  • While text chat is working for sending/receiving messages, the text chat indicator above the talk button will be blinking.
  • If you click the "Help" button at the right bottom corner, you will get short cut help to copy and paste in the text chat boxes.
