  • You can write or draw on the whiteboard at the right top of the application.
  • If you click anywhere in the whiteboard title tool bar, whiteboard tools will be expanded (shared display will be hidden).
  • Tools Description:
    - draw free lines.
    - draw straight line.
    - type text.
    - click on the object that you want to cut to erase one.
    - click to erase all that on the whiteboard.
  • Tools Description (continue):
    - select to edit text, shape or lines.
    - draw oval or circle.
    - draw a rectangular shape.
    - draw straight lines.
  • right side of a tool means that it is expandable to give selection of styles of a shape, line or text.

  • You can cut, copy, paste, uncut, or arrange the whiteboard contents by right clicking after selecting the object.
