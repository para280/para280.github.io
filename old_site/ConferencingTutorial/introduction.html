  • Before start or join a meeting, Configure application and audio first.
  • If you click join button to start or join a meeting, Anabas Collaboration Application will be automatically loaded.
  • When the application is successfully loaded, you will see the windows that look like the left image.
  • The window is composed with a white board, shared display and voice chat/text chat.
  • Participants will be displayed under whiteboard and shared display tool bars.
  • Your name will be displayed at the top of participants panel.
  • The host of the meeting will be displayed with violet color and participants will be displayed with pale purple.
  • Red participants are "participants having trouble", and yellow participants are "participants have question".
  • If you have a new message recieved, the number of instant messages that you received will be appear besides of your name.
  • The "Switch View" button is to switch participant view between auditorium view(on stage) and list view(off stage).
